Breakfast Recipt

Welcome to China.We always eat healthy and simple breakfast in morning.Follow the recipt to make your own breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1 or more eggs
  • 1 cup milk or soy milk
  • 1 bowl congee
  • 1 or 2 youtiao

Instructions

  1. Get up.
  2. Go to the nearest breakfast house.
  3. Buy the Ingredients above.
  4. Eat here or back home.
